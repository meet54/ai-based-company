# Walkgether

## Tagline

**Walk Together. Stay Healthy. Build Connections.**

---

# Project Overview

Walkgether is a social fitness platform designed to connect people through walking. The application enables users to discover nearby walking partners and communities based on shared interests, schedules, locations, and fitness goals.

The goal of Walkgether is to transform walking from a solitary activity into a social experience that promotes both physical well-being and meaningful human connections.

---

# Problem Statement

Modern lifestyles have led to increasing levels of physical inactivity and social isolation. Many people struggle to maintain consistent exercise routines due to a lack of motivation, accountability, or companionship. At the same time, individuals are seeking genuine relationships beyond digital interactions.

There is currently no mainstream platform specifically focused on connecting people through walking while prioritizing safety, community engagement, and wellness.

---

# Solution

Walkgether addresses these challenges by providing a platform where users can:

* Find nearby walking partners.
* Join local walking groups and communities.
* Organize scheduled walks.
* Build healthy habits through accountability.
* Develop real-world friendships through shared activities.
* Track their walking progress and achievements.

---

# Mission

To inspire healthier lifestyles and stronger communities by making walking a shared experience.

---

# Vision

To become the world's leading platform that connects people through movement, friendship, and well-being.

---

# Core Features

## 1. User Authentication

* Sign up using email, phone number, Google, or Apple accounts.
* Secure login functionality.
* OTP verification.
* Password reset options.

## 2. User Profiles

* Profile picture and personal details.
* Interests and hobbies.
* Walking preferences.
* Fitness goals.
* Availability schedules.
* Privacy settings.

## 3. Location-Based Discovery

* Nearby user discovery.
* Distance filters.
* Location permissions management.
* Interactive map integration.

## 4. Walk Matching System

Users can find compatible walking partners based on:

* Location proximity.
* Preferred walking time.
* Walking speed.
* Shared interests.
* Fitness objectives.

## 5. Walking Groups

* Create public groups.
* Create private groups.
* Join existing groups.
* Group management capabilities.
* Group announcements.

## 6. Walk Scheduling

* Schedule one-time walks.
* Create recurring walking events.
* Invite friends or group members.
* Calendar synchronization.
* Automated reminders.

## 7. Messaging System

* One-to-one messaging.
* Group chats.
* Media sharing support.
* Notification integration.

## 8. Activity Tracking

* Step counting.
* Distance tracking.
* Walking duration tracking.
* Historical activity records.
* Personal statistics dashboard.

## 9. Notifications

* Match notifications.
* Event reminders.
* Group activity alerts.
* Chat notifications.

## 10. Safety Features

* User reporting system.
* User blocking functionality.
* Verified profiles.
* Emergency contact options.

## 11. Admin Dashboard

* User management.
* Report handling.
* Community moderation.
* Analytics and insights.
